  1. 1. Part 1 (b) household energy usage My TRIZ competition
  2. 2. The problem statement The problem of energy usage face by each family Price increase in energy usage Electric appliances is necessary to maintain the comfort of the house, hence, increase energy usage With theses problem introduced, cost of living for each family increased.
  3. 3. Benefits If the problem is solved: Cost of living decrease Minimise the burden of environment Impact If the problem is solved: Less energy generation, thus more environmental friendly Quality of air are maintain
  4. 4. TRIZ process Problem definition Solution implementation Solution generation Root cause identification Evaluation Solve the problem? No Yes Closed
  5. 5. Problem definition: How to reduce the power usage without using much money Root cause : Heat lost of air conditioning, dim rooms and rooms too messy
